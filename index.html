Want to be part of our developers community?

"Coding is a learned skill that anyone can acquire" - LORENA MESA

"Learning to code is useful no matter what your career ambitions are" - ARIANNA HUFFINGTON

01.

Register as a developer on our Developers community.

Our Developers coding program covers alot of topics across the tech industry. Join people from all over the world on this life-changing tech journey.

02.

Pick a career track you'll like to explore.

Our online developers courses are meant for those who love tech. As part of learning, students are divided into groups and are expected to work on their final projects. They bring to life what they've been learning by working on real-life projects.

03.

Complete all projects and get certified!

As part of the program, participants complete short tasks and are expected to complete their capstone project at the end of the learning period. Working on such projects helps bring to life what they've been learning. At the end of the learning period, they receive certificates both from Cregitech and our learning partners.

04.

Become part of our Developers Network.

All certified developers then become members of Cregitech Developers Network. There they become part of our mentorship program, they get firsthand information of job vacancies, they meet other creative developers, work on projects and also an opportunity to change the world too.
